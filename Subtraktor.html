⚠️ More Information: SD Card Setup & Transcoder Details ▼
📂 Mandatory Tonverk Engine SD Card Placement:
  • Extract the downloaded .zip package directly to your Tonverk SD card [3.4.4].
  • The final path to the folder on your SD card must look like: User/Drum Sets/MyCustomKit/ [3.4.4]
  • This folder will hold both your .eldrum file and the associated converted .wav files flatly together inside it [3.4.4].
🎵 Built-in Sample Transcoder Details:
  • Accepted Input Formats: WAV, MP3, FLAC, AIFF, M4A, OGG, AAC, OPUS. Drag and drop any of these directly.
  • The Conversion Process: Every sample is parsed in-browser and converted to a standardized 16-bit, 48,000 Hz, Stereo Interleaved LPCM WAV inside the exported ZIP [3.4.4].
  • Why this is ideal: Tonverk's hardware playback engine runs natively at 48 kHz [3.4.4]. Standardizing files beforehand guarantees click-free playback, minimizes hardware resampling latencies, and avoids pitch shifting artifacts [3.4.4].
  • Duplicates Guard: Files with identical sanitized names are automatically skipped during drag-and-drop imports to prevent repeating entries in your pool.
